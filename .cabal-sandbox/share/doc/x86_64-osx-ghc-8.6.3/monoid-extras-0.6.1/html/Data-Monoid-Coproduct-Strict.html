Coproduct

data m :+: n #

m :+: n is the coproduct of monoids m and n. Concatentation is equivilent to

(m1 :+: n1) <> (m2 :+: n2) = (m1 <> m2) :+: (n1 <> act m1 n2)@

but has a more efficient internal implimentation.
