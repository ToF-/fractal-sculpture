class Bifunctor p => Assoc p where #

"Semigroup-y" Bifunctors.

assoc . unassoc = id
unassoc . assoc = id
assoc . bimap (bimap f g) h = bimap f (bimap g h) . assoc

This library doesn't provide Monoidal class, with left and right unitors. Are they useful in practice?
